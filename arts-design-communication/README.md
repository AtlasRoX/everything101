## 📌 Scope

This category covers the following subjects:
- **Art:** Art history, artistic movements, techniques, mediums, and famous creators.
- **Music:** Music theory, scales, instrumentation, genres, and composition.
- **Design:** Graphic design, user experience (UX) design, product design, and visual principles.
- **Architecture:** Architectural styles, engineering constraints, and historical structures.
- **Writing:** Creative writing, structured essays, storytelling frameworks, and copywriting.
- **Communication:** Public speaking, body language, interpersonal communication, and rhetoric.
