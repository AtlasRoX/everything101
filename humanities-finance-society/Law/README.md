# Law Portal ⚖️

Welcome to the **Law Portal** of Everything101.

Law is the system of rules that a society or government develops to deal with crime, business agreements, social relations, and civil order. It is the framework that allows millions of strangers to coordinate, resolve conflicts, and protect human dignity peacefully.

This portal provides **15 beginner-friendly guides** to help you master the fundamentals of law.
