# History Portal 🌍

Welcome to the **History Portal** of Everything101! 

History is not just a list of dead kings, dry dates, and memorized battles. It is a detective story. It is the study of how human choices, accidents, ideas, and forces shaped the world we live in today. Understanding history is like looking at the source code of our modern civilization—it explains why our laws, cities, economies, and conflicts exist in the form they do.

Here you will find **29 beginner-friendly guides** written to help you grasp the core concepts of history in minutes, complete with clear analogies and structured explanations.
