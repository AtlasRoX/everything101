# Finance Portal 💰

Welcome to the **Finance Portal** of Everything101.

Finance is the science of money management, capital allocation, and risk. It spans from your day-to-day personal budgeting and savings to complex investment markets, banking operations, and the digital frontier of cryptocurrencies.

This portal provides **23 beginner-friendly guides** to help you master the fundamentals of finance.
