# Philosophy Portal 📜

Welcome to the **Philosophy Portal** of Everything101! 

Philosophy is not about memorizing dusty facts; it is the active practice of asking fundamental questions about our existence, our minds, our values, and the universe. It is the tool we use to dismantle confusion and build clear, first-principles understanding.

Here you will find **42 beginner-friendly guides** written to help you grasp the core concepts of philosophy in minutes, complete with relatable analogies and visual diagrams.
