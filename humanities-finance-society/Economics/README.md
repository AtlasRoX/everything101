# Economics Portal 📊

Welcome to the **Economics Portal** of Everything101.

Economics is the study of scarcity, choice, and how societies allocate their limited resources to meet unlimited wants. It answers the fundamental questions of human livelihood and coordination: *How are prices determined? What drives economic growth? How do markets fail, and what is the government's role in fixing them?*

Here you will find **21 beginner-friendly guides** that take you from the basic laws of supply and demand to complex macroeconomic systems, global trade, and economic development.
