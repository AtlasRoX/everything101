# Human Geography Portal 🌍

Welcome to the **Human Geography Portal** of Everything101.

Human geography is the study of how humans interact with, shape, and are shaped by the Earth's surfaces. It explores the spatial patterns of human populations, cultures, migration, cities, agriculture, economies, and political systems, helping us understand the complex web of interactions that define our globalized world.

This portal provides **11 beginner-friendly guides** to help you master the fundamentals of human geography.
