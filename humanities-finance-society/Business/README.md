# Business Portal 🏢

Welcome to the **Business Portal** of Everything101.

Business is the engine of value creation and exchange in society. It is the study of how people organize themselves to solve customer problems, build products, compete in markets, and sustain operations.

Whether you want to understand how a startup scales, how corporate balance sheets work, or how to negotiate a win-win contract, this portal provides **20 beginner-friendly guides** to help you master the fundamentals of business.
