# Psychology Portal 🧠

Welcome to the **Psychology Portal** of Everything101.

Psychology is the scientific study of the mind and behavior. It sits at the intersection of biology, philosophy, medicine, and social science — answering the questions that matter most: *Why do we feel what we feel? Why do we do what we do? How can we think and live better?*

Here you will find **27 beginner-friendly guides** that take you from the physical structure of the brain to the deepest questions about creativity, intelligence, and mental health.
