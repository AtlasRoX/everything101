# Political Science Portal 🏛️

Welcome to the **Political Science Portal** of Everything101.

Political Science is the scientific study of power, governance, institutions, and political behavior. It answers the fundamental questions of human organization: *Who gets what, when, and how? How should societies make collective decisions? What is the balance between individual liberty and state authority?*

Here you will find **18 beginner-friendly guides** that range from the basic mechanics of governments and constitutions to the major ideologies and global diplomacy.
