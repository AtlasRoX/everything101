## 📌 Scope

This category covers the following subjects:
- **Mathematics & Calculus:** Arithmetic, algebra, geometry, calculus, and mathematical concepts.
- **Statistics & Data Science:** Probabilities, statistical models, and data analytics.
- **Physics:** Classical mechanics, thermodynamics, quantum physics, relativity, and electromagnetism.
- **Chemistry:** Elements, chemical reactions, molecular bonds, and organic chemistry.
- **Biology:** Genetics, cell biology, evolution, ecology, and biological systems.
- **Medicine:** Anatomy, physiology, common diseases, pharmacology, and healthcare systems.
- **Astronomy:** Galaxies, stars, planets, cosmic phenomena, and space exploration.
